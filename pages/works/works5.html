Artist’s Brain with audio

4’21’’ Single channel video with stereo audio on headphones. 2014. This work has been shown at Ormond Studios, Dublin,2014, Laban Center, London, 2014 and MEX Symposium Maynooth 2015

A MRI Scan of the artists’ brain is layered with recorded thoughts by the artist. MRI scan of artist’s brain courtesy of Institute of Neuroscience Trinity College Dublin.
